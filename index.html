MAX RESTAURANT

At Max Restaurant, we pride ourselves on using all local, seasonal, ingredients, including pasture-raised meat from Porter Road Butcher to make our Appalachian heritage inspired barbecue. Our meats are dry-rubbed and smoked with native Tennessee cherry until tender and juicy. No dry meat here. All sides are handmade in small batches using locally grown produce.
